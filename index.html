What can you do in practice?

Share your work

  • for code:
    • GitHub
    • GitLab
    • Bitbucket
  • for data:
    • figshare
    • zenodo
    • Dryad
  • for anything:
    • Google Docs
    • your own website

Share your results

  • use unique identifiers
    • ORCID
    • ResearcherID / Publons
  • publish in OA journals
  • use preprint servers
  • consider open review both as author and reviewers

Communicate with public

  • use social networks
  • use virtual social networks
  • put your work in wider context
